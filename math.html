About this Page

This is the third section in this website. There are series of posts covering a wide spectrum of free resources on different subject areas.Today we are providing you with a list of some of the best free Math websites where you can have access to a plethora of Math lesson plans, free printables, games, activities, video tutorials and many more. This section of website provides PreK-12 educators with access to free digital content and professional development opportunities designed to improve teacher effectiveness and student achievement.

Khanacademy

Khan Academy allows you to learn almost anything for free. There are over 10,000 videos and explanations at your fingertips in math, science, economics, history, and much, much more. Also, you can bookmark your favorite content to “Your List” for easy access.

School Yourself

At School Yourself, there are reinvented online education in the style of 1-on-1 learning. There are hundreds of interactive, personalized math lessons, using a custom analytics platform to continuously improve them. Math Planet

Math planet is an online resource where one can study math for free. They offer high school math courses, such as Pre-algebra, Algebra 1, Algebra 2 and Geometry.They also have practice tests for the SAT and ACT.The educational material is focused on US high school maths. However, since maths is the same all over the world, this website welcomes everybody to study math for free.
